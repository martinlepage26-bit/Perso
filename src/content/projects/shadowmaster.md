SHADOWMASTER is the red-team instrument for the AI Governance Master Project. It proceeds by systematically inverting every governance principle: intake rules become intake theater, provenance requirements become provenance performance, audit trails become audit furniture.

The protocol does not attack from outside the governance language. It works from inside it — colonizing each control with its adversarial twin and testing whether the original control has enough structural specificity to survive. Controls that collapse under SHADOWMASTER pressure are redesigned. Controls that hold are confirmed as genuinely binding rather than merely legible.
