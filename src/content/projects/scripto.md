*Scripto* is the separate app line for archive intake, document sorting, cross-reference reporting, and masterlist generation inside the `Agency` repository.

Its current desktop surface is *Dr. Sort-Academic Helper*. The important distinction is that Scripto is not Lotus: it handles document organization and reporting workflows, while Lotus handles social agency scoring for notes and other local text material.

Like Lotus, Scripto is local-first rather than SaaS. The site should point clearly to the app line and its source, while the actual working surface remains honest about being desktop-first software.
