How it works

A seasonal method, not a natal chart.

Astral renders repeatable seasonal readings from the book's sign calendar and vocabulary. It does not calculate planets, houses, or transits.

1. Entry input

Astral starts with either a direct sign choice or a month-and-day date used to resolve the seasonal sign range.

2. Seasonal grounding

The app maps that date to the sign calendar already defined for the book. It does not use birth time, birth place, or planetary positions.

3. Reading frame

Each sign is read through the same six fields: key note, season mood, body feeling, movement pattern, shadow, and ritual.

4. Rendered output

Astral turns those fields into a summary, a longer message, and grounded guidance lines that stay close to earthly life.

What it does not do

No hidden astronomy claims.

  • No birth time or birth place input.
  • No planetary positions, houses, aspects, or transit layers.
  • No claim that the book needs a natal chart engine in order to speak.

If full charts come later

That would be a separate build step.

To render full natal gaialogys later, the project would need a declared gaialogy system, real astronomical calculations or an external chart engine, and additional birth inputs such as exact time and place.

The current Astral app is intentionally narrower: a seasonal reading surface grounded in Breath of the Astral Year.
