Archive logic

Why this section exists.

Projects preserves visibility into active work while keeping publication and governance claims disciplined.

  • Method-facing tools stay visible here while they are being tested and refined.
  • Book-stage work remains here until it reaches published-book status.
  • Apps in development remain public without implying production readiness.
  • Unpublished papers stay here until publication status and bibliographic metadata are verified.
