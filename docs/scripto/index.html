Deterministic writing app

A control surface for manuscripts under pressure.

SCRIPTO is not a text generator. It is a packet builder that forces every draft through an explicit sequence: intake, recursive testing, control assignment, and bounded export.

The objective is simple and strict: make claims travel only as far as their visible evidence trail can carry them.
