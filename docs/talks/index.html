October 1, 2025

Public lecture

Sorcellerie, paganisme et communautés 2SLGBTQ+

Semaine de sensibilisation à la diversité sexuelle et à l'égalité des genres · Cégep du Vieux Montréal, Montreal

A public lecture on contemporary paganism as part of broader modern spiritualities, with attention to how 2SLGBTQ+ communities negotiate gender representations, authenticity, and belonging within pagan practice. Paganism's unstructured, individual-meaning-centred character makes it especially available for queer reinterpretation and ritual innovation — a pattern documented across Montreal's pagan community.

July 2015

Conference presentation

Religiosités queer néo-païennes et la question de l'authenticité

ISSR 33rd International Conference — Sensing Religion · UCLouvain, Louvain-la-Neuve, Belgium

A conference presentation examining the question of authenticity within queer neo-pagan religious practice. Draws on ethnographic fieldwork in Montreal's pagan community to analyse how practitioners negotiate authentic identities against the background of fluid gender norms, non-institutional spiritual forms, and queer experience. The paper developed into the 2018 Religiologiques article of the same name.
